Grimace Shake

Description

A mysterious purple concotion of uncertain origin. Drink at your own risk.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup frozen blueberries
  • 1/2 cup raspberries
  • 3 scoops vanilla ice cream
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • whipped cream
  • 1/2 teaspoon blue spirulina
  • sprinkles

Steps

  1. Let the ice cream sit out for several minutes to soften.
  2. Add the blueberries, raspberries, ice cream, milk, and blue spirulina to a blender. Blend into a homogenous mixture.
  3. Pour into a large glass. Garnish with whipped cream and sprinkles.
