Toast Sandwich

Description

A stale of Victorian invalids. Food doesn't get any simpler (or cheaper) than this.

Ingredients

  • 3 slices of bread
  • butter
  • salt
  • pepper

Steps

  1. Toast and butter one slice of bread.
  2. If you're feeling daring, garnish the toast with salt and pepper.
  3. Place the toast in between the two remaining slices of bread and "enjoy".
